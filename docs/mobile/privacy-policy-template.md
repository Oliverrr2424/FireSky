# FireSky Privacy Policy (Template)

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Data We Process
- **Approximate/precise location**: used only to fetch sunrise/sunset and weather forecasts for your selected area.
- **Search query text**: city names entered by users for geocoding.
- **Diagnostic logs**: local runtime errors for troubleshooting (if enabled by the app owner).
- **Account data**: when you choose Google sign-in or email/password registration, we store the account identifier, email address, display name, and Google avatar (if supplied) to provide your account. Passwords are stored only as salted password hashes.
- **Cloud-synced content**: saved locations, camera viewpoints, forecast-change history, forecast feedback, and alert preferences.
- **Push token**: a device notification token when you opt in to background alerts.

## How We Use Data
- Deliver forecast results and map visualizations.
- Improve reliability and prevent abuse of backend APIs.
- Synchronize the features you explicitly save across your signed-in devices and deliver opted-in weather alerts.

## Data Sharing
- Forecast and air-quality data are requested from Open-Meteo and project-owned APIs.
- We do not sell personal data.

## Data Retention
- Client-side cache is stored in app/browser storage and can expire automatically.
- Account data remains until you delete your account. Define a documented retention period for inactive accounts and diagnostic logs before release.

## Your Controls
- You can deny location permission and search cities manually.
- You can clear app data to remove local cache.
- You can sign out at any time. The in-app **Delete account and cloud data** control permanently removes account-scoped cloud data.

## Contact
- Add publisher email and support URL before submission.
