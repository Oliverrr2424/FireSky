# FireSky Privacy Policy (Template)

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Data We Process
- **Approximate/precise location**: used only to fetch sunrise/sunset and weather forecasts for your selected area.
- **Search query text**: city names entered by users for geocoding.
- **Diagnostic logs**: local runtime errors for troubleshooting (if enabled by the app owner).

## How We Use Data
- Deliver forecast results and map visualizations.
- Improve reliability and prevent abuse of backend APIs.

## Data Sharing
- Forecast and air-quality data are requested from Open-Meteo and project-owned APIs.
- We do not sell personal data.

## Data Retention
- Client-side cache is stored in app/browser storage and can expire automatically.
- Server retention policy should be defined by the deploy owner.

## Your Controls
- You can deny location permission and search cities manually.
- You can clear app data to remove local cache.

## Contact
- Add publisher email and support URL before submission.
