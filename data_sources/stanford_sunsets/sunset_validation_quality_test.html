Pictures used in the test given to human research assistants to assess whether days with many sunset posts did in fact have better sunsets. Each row contains one pair of sunsets in a city, one sampled from a day with many sunset posts and one sampled from a day with an average number of sunset posts, displayed in random order. Research assistants were asked to choose which sunset they thought was more beautiful (without being shown which was sampled from the day with more posts) and overwhelmingly preferred the sunsets on days with many posts.
